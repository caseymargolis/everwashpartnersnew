As we approach the end of another incredible year, it’s time to reflect on the milestones and achievements that made 2024 unforgettable for EverWash and our partners. From groundbreaking updates to our platform, strategic acquisitions, exciting partnerships, and industry recognition, we’ve continued to push the envelope in helping car wash operators thrive. Let’s take a look back at what we accomplished together.

### A Fresh Start: EverWash’s Major App Update

*February 6, 2024*: We kicked off the year with a major update to the EverWash app, designed to elevate both the car wash experience and operational efficiency for our partners. This update introduced new features that improved usability, expanded data insights, and made managing memberships easier than ever. The goal? To make every wash operator and customer interaction seamless and rewarding.

[Read More](https://www.everwash.com/newsroom/tue-feb-06-2024-15-55-17-gmt-0500-eastern-standard-time-everwash-unveils-major-app-update-to-elevate-car-wash-experience-and-operational-efficiency/)

### Expanding Horizons: The Acquisition of Coinless

*May 13, 2024*: In May, we announced the acquisition of Coinless, a move that bolstered our technology and service capabilities. This acquisition allowed EverWash to offer expanded mobile payment and loyalty solutions, paving the way for greater innovation across the car wash industry.

[Read More](https://www.everwash.com/newsroom/mon-may-13-2024-09-30-51-gmt-0500-central-daylight-time-everwash-announces-acquisition-of-coinless-expanding-its-technology-and-service-capabilities-in-the-car-wash-industry/)

### Enhancing Training with ICA’s LEAD Program

*July 15, 2024*: We were thrilled to partner with the International Carwash Association’s LEAD Program to enhance training opportunities for car wash operators and their teams. This collaboration reinforced our commitment to supporting wash operators through professional development and skill-building resources.

[Read More](https://www.everwash.com/newsroom/mon-jul-15-2024-11-21-58-gmt-0400-eastern-daylight-time-international-carwash-associations-lead-program-partners-with-everwash-to-enhance-member-training/)

### Webinars: Sharing Insights to Drive Success

2024 was a landmark year for EverWash in sharing knowledge and expertise with our partners. We hosted and produced more webinar content than ever before, delivering actionable insights through our **Rocket Webinar Series**, **Master Class Sessions**, and **Data Drips** videos. From strategies for growing membership programs to innovative marketing techniques, these sessions were designed to empower car wash operators with the tools and confidence to succeed.

Here’s a closer look at some of this year’s standout webinars:

* [Boosting Membership Sales: ](https://www.everwash.com/blog/2024-03-07-watch-insights-from-everwashs-latest-webinar-on-boosting-membership-sales/)This session focused on proven techniques to increase membership sign-ups and retain existing members. From effective signage to leveraging in-app promotions, we provided practical, data-backed strategies to help operators turn occasional visitors into loyal members.
* [Secrets to Increasing Car Wash Traffic: ](https://www.everwash.com/blog/2024-05-22-watch-secrets-to-increasing-car-wash-traffic-webinar-recap/)EverWash experts shared key insights into driving more traffic to car washes, including the use of targeted campaigns, partnerships, and local marketing strategies. Attendees left with actionable tips to bring in more customers and increase their wash counts.
* [Build Your Perfect Car Wash Team: ](https://www.everwash.com/blog/2024-11-06-in-case-you-missed-it-build-your-perfect-car-wash-team-webinar/)This webinar explored the importance of building a strong, motivated team to support membership success. From hiring practices to employee training, attendees learned how to create a team culture that drives retention and member satisfaction.

Additionally, our [Data Drips](https://www.everwash.com/blog/2024-07-15-data-drip-episode-1-member-focused-promotional-campaigns/) video series debuted this year, delivering bite-sized insights on topics like member-focused promotional campaigns. These episodes, along with the rest of our webinar content, showcased EverWash’s commitment to helping operators succeed with smarter, data-driven solutions.
