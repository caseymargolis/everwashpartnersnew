## How to Leverage Data Analytics to Drive Growth

### **Track Key Membership Metrics**

Your membership program’s success hinges on more than just the number of sign-ups. Dive deeper with metrics that reveal real performance.

**Metrics to Monitor:**

* **Active Members**: Keep an eye on growth trends and churn rates.
* **Average Usage Rate**: Understand how often members are using your services.
* **Lifetime Value (LTV):** Calculate the total revenue each member brings over their subscription period.
* **Net Member Change**: Track how many members you gain versus lose each month.
