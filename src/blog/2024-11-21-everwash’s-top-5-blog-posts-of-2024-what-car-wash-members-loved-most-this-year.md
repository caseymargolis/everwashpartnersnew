As the year comes to a close, it’s the perfect time to reflect on what captivated EverWash members in 2024. Our blog is full of tips, insights, and fun topics for car lovers—and this year, a few posts really stood out! These articles reveal what car wash members are curious about, from practical advice to pop culture nostalgia.

For car wash operators, this list offers valuable insight into what engages your members and keeps them coming back for more. Here are the top 5 blog articles that car wash members loved most in 2024:

**1. [Car Wash Essentials: What You Should Know Before Your First Visit](https://www.everwash.com/blog/2024-01-15-car-wash-essentials-what-you-should-know-before-your-first-visit/)**

This practical guide for first-time car wash visitors was our most-read article of the year! It breaks down everything a customer needs to know to make the most of their car wash experience, from prepping their vehicle to understanding the different wash types. The popularity of this post highlights that many members are looking for guidance to feel confident and comfortable during their visits.

**2. [Top Car Websites and Blogs to Follow for News, Reviews, and Culture](https://www.everwash.com/blog/2022-10-11-top-car-websites-and-blogs-to-follow-for-news-reviews-and-culture/)**

Even two years after its original publication, this evergreen article continues to attract readers. Featuring a curated list of the best car-related websites, it’s clear that car enthusiasts love staying connected with the latest news, reviews, and trends. For operators, this shows the value of tapping into car culture to engage with your members.

**3. [How Often Should You Really Wash Your Car?](https://www.everwash.com/blog/2024-07-30-how-often-should-you-really-wash-your-car/)**

It’s a question every car owner has asked, and this article provides the definitive answer based on factors like climate, driving habits, and vehicle type. Members found this post especially useful for planning their wash schedules, showing that customers value expert advice on keeping their vehicles in top condition.

**4. [Top 10 Most Famous Cars from Television History](https://www.everwash.com/blog/2024-01-09-top-10-most-famous-cars-from-television-history/)**

From KITT in Knight Rider to the Batmobile, this nostalgia-fueled post brought joy to readers of all ages. It’s a great reminder that entertainment and storytelling can be just as powerful as practical content when building customer connections.

**5. [How a Car Wash Membership Boosts Your Health and Happiness](https://www.everwash.com/blog/2024-01-09-how-a-car-wash-membership-boosts-your-health-and-happiness/)**

This feel-good piece explored the surprising ways a clean car contributes to physical and mental well-being, from reducing allergens to providing a sense of accomplishment. Members loved learning how their car wash membership isn’t just good for their car—it’s good for them, too!
